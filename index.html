ICFAI Law School

The ICFAI Law school hosts simulated environmental court proceedings, impromptu constitutional debates, sustainability showcases, environmental film screenings, mediation workshops, legal innovation sessions, and sociopolitical cartoon exhibitions. Additionally, the school conducts moot court competitions, human rights awareness campaigns, and workshops on emerging legal technologies. These activities are designed to enhance legal acumen, promote ethical reasoning, and equip students with the skills to address contemporary legal and environmental challenges.

Faculty Of Science & Technology

The ICFAI Technology school organizes hackathons, robotics competitions, coding marathons, and tech innovation showcases. In addition, the school hosts workshops on artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cybersecurity, as well as seminars on the ethical implications of emerging technologies. Students also participate in collaborative projects with industry partners, prototype development challenges, and tech-driven social impact initiatives. These activities focus on fostering technical expertise, encouraging innovation, and equipping students to tackle real-world problems through technology.

ICFAI Business School

The ICFAI Management school offers marketing strategy simulations, mock auctions, business ideation pitches, comprehensive problem-solving sessions, crisis management drills, general knowledge quizzes, and market dynamics challenges. Furthermore, the school organizes leadership summits, entrepreneurial boot camps, and networking events with industry experts. Students also engage in case study analyses and global business simulations, which aim to sharpen managerial skills, foster innovation, and prepare them for the complexities of the corporate world.
